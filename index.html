Privacy & Policy

Taskly Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025
Last Updated: December 2025

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

At Taskly, we believe your task data is personal and private. We've designed Taskly from the ground up to respect your privacy. Unlike other task managers, we don't collect, analyze, or sell your data. Everything stays on your device.

2. What Information We Collect (Hint: Almost Nothing)

Local Storage Only: Taskly stores the following information exclusively in your browser's localStorage:

  • Tasks ‚Äì Title, description, due date, priority, reminders
  • Profile Information ‚Äì Name, email (for login), profile picture, bio
  • App Settings ‚Äì Theme preference, notification settings
  • Archive ‚Äì Archived tasks (stored locally for 30 days)
  • Authentication ‚Äì Login credentials (encrypted in localStorage)

Important: We don't have servers collecting this data. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide Taskly's functionality:

  • Task Display ‚Äì Showing your tasks in the dashboard
  • Reminders ‚Äì Browser notifications for upcoming tasks
  • Personalization ‚Äì Applying your chosen theme and settings
  • Profile Management ‚Äì Displaying your name and photo
  • Search ‚Äì Filtering tasks based on your search queries

4. Data Storage & Retention

Storage Location: All data is stored in your browser's localStorage.

Retention Period:

  • Active tasks: Stored until you delete or archive them
  • Archived tasks: Automatically cleared after 30 days
  • Profile data: Stored until you delete your account
  • Settings: Persist until you change or clear them

Data Deletion: You can delete all data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Taskly uses browser localStorage (not cookies) for:

  • Session Management ‚Äì Keeping you logged in during your session
  • "Remember Me" Feature ‚Äì Saving your email for convenience
  • Theme Preferences ‚Äì Remembering your chosen theme
  • Task Data ‚Äì Storing all your tasks and settings

You can clear localStorage at any time through your browser settings or using Taskly's "Clear All Data" feature.

6. Third-Party Services

Google Sign-In Simulation: The current version simulates Google authentication for demonstration purposes. In a production environment, this would integrate with Firebase Authentication, which would have its own privacy policy.

No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking services.

No Ad Networks: Taskly is completely ad-free.

7. Data Export & Backup

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Export all your data as a JSON file via Settings
  • Backup: Regular exports serve as your backup system
  • Portability: Your data is in standard JSON format for easy migration

8. Security Measures

While Taskly stores data locally, we implement several security practices:

  • Password Protection ‚Äì Passwords are stored encrypted in localStorage
  • Browser Security ‚Äì Leverages browser's built-in security features
  • Input Validation ‚Äì All user inputs are sanitized to prevent injection attacks
  • Session Management ‚Äì Proper session handling prevents unauthorized access

Note: For maximum security, we recommend using Taskly on your personal devices only.

9. Children's Privacy

Taskly is not specifically designed for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally, parents have full visibility and control over their children's data.

10. Your Rights & Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Correction: Edit tasks and profile information anytime
  • Deletion: Delete individual tasks or all data at once
  • Export: Download your complete data package
  • Opt-out: Stop using the app and clear localStorage

11. Policy Changes

We will update this privacy policy if we add new features that change how we handle data. Since Taskly is a local-first app, significant changes to data handling will be clearly communicated within the app.

12. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or data access requests:

Email: mrongrowdro23@gmail.com
Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

13. Legal Compliance

Taskly is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR Principles ‚Äì Data minimization, purpose limitation, storage limitation
  • CCPA Spirit ‚Äì Right to know, delete, and opt-out
  • Privacy by Design ‚Äì Privacy considerations from the initial design phase
